Collaborative 45. From WordPress and email to an integrated HubSpot hiring system

Author: Irene Florentín Aragón

1. Context

The new Isaval Global corporate website is being built entirely inside HubSpot to reduce external dependencies and take advantage of Content Enterprise together with CRM, HubDB, workflows and AI capabilities.

2. Initial setup

Job posts were published in WordPress through manual entries and simple forms. Applications arrived by email, with no connected CRM, no candidate traceability, no pipeline and a highly manual workflow.

3. Objective

Design an integrated system able to publish dynamic job posts, manage applications automatically, centralize information in the CRM and simplify HR work without losing operational simplicity.

4. Built solution

The project connects HubDB, dynamic pages, forms, CRM contacts, a custom applications object and a hiring pipeline that tracks each application from entry to resolution.

5. Role of AI

AI acted as a real support layer for architecture, programming, issue solving and functional design, making it possible to deliver the build with internal resources and lower third-party dependence.

6. Impact

HR gains traceability, centralization and automation. Marketing integrates talent into the corporate digital ecosystem. The company gains technological autonomy and a scalable base for future HubSpot developments.
