Collaborative 45 · From WordPress and email to an integrated HubSpot hiring system

Author: Irene Florentín Aragón
Irene Florentín proposes turning job-post publication and candidate management into an integrated HubSpot system, adding automation, traceability and new internal autonomy for HR and marketing.

Starting point

Job-post publication and candidate intake relied on WordPress, simple forms and email. There was no connected CRM, no hiring pipeline and no real candidate traceability across the process.

This was not treated as a technical migration only. It was used as an opportunity to redesign a critical internal process from end to end.

What the project had to solve

Dependency

The workflow depended on external tools, manual tasks and handcrafted reuse of previous content.

Traceability

Applications arrived by email without context, history or a structured recruitment pipeline.

Scalability

The system was hard to expand and fragile whenever volume increased or the process changed.

Autonomy

HR and marketing needed an environment that kept things simple while radically improving the underlying system.

The solution built

The project was developed entirely in HubSpot to create an autonomous corporate web environment, connecting dynamic job-post publication, forms, CRM, a custom applications object and a visual hiring pipeline.

Posts are fed from HubDB, automatically generate dynamic pages and allow each application to create its contact, its application record and its direct entry into the HR pipeline.

How AI contributed

Architecture

AI helped design the functional logic, the data structure and the relationship between modules and automations.

Development

It acted as a real technical support layer to program custom modules, solve issues and accelerate deployment.

Autonomy

It made it possible to deliver a complex build with internal resources, reducing both external and internal dependencies.

Future scale

It leaves a base ready for scoring, classification, reporting and new automation layers inside HubSpot.

What Isaval gains

HR gains a more visual, traceable and centralized process. Marketing integrates the talent area into the corporate digital ecosystem. And the company gains technological autonomy, future evolution capacity and a practical demonstration of how AI can accelerate internal developments with real impact.

Executive conclusion: this pill turns a web migration into an operational transformation lever, connecting talent, CRM, automation and AI on top of a scalable base that did not exist before.
