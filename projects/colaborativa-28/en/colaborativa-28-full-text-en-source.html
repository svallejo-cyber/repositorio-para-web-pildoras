From rebuilding the model to scaling it: Spain–Portugal sales consolidation without duplicating measures

Context

Budget preparation for 2026 required a consolidated Spain–Portugal sales view, not only separate legal-entity analysis.

The Power BI model already contained sales, kilos, units, order lines and nearly 200 measures used across operational reports.

Identified problem

The model could analyze Spain and Portugal separately, but it had no reliable consolidated option.

A direct sum would create duplication because Spain includes sales to Portugal’s internal customer.

Editing around 200 measures one by one would have taken days or weeks and created major testing risk.

What has been built

The solution was to add an extra logic layer in Tabular Editor without touching the existing measures.

Users can now select Spain, Portugal or Consolidated, and the consolidated view excludes the internal cross-company distortion automatically.

Role of AI

AI helped identify an alternative to manual measure-by-measure work, introduced Tabular Editor as the right tool and helped solve implementation issues quickly.

Without that support, the natural path would have been a far slower manual rewrite.

Operational result and conclusion

The consolidated view is already built, validated and available in the ANÁLISIS VENTAS report.

The value lies not in changing more, but in building a model that can scale without being rebuilt.
