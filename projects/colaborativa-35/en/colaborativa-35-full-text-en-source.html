From manual Excel reconciliation in Excel to one-click automation

The problem

Reconciling bank statements against ERP records relied on pivot tables, manual file crossing, and on-screen visual validation. It was slow, error-prone, and hard to scale.

What this initiative builds

The initiative proposes a web application that standardizes both files, matches debits and credits, separates exact matches from differences, and lets the team manage issues from a single interface.

Business value

EFFICIENCY

Processes that used to take hours are resolved in seconds.

PRECISION

It removes much of the human error inherent in manual visual reconciliation.

VISIBILITY

It surfaces matches, missing items, and pending differences from a single view.

CONTROL

It restores traceability to a key finance workflow.

Conclusion

Lenin Cabrera moves bank reconciliation away from a manual operational burden toward a faster, more accurate, and more traceable financial control workflow, freeing time for analysis instead of cell checking.
