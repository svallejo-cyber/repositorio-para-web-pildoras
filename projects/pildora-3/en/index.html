Executive Committee - Isaval

Pill 4 - DATA

Executive summary to accelerate AI adoption among non-IT profiles

Context

The organization already shows clear reactions: IT is moving very fast, management shows different adoption rhythms, and a second layer is starting to produce first pilot cases.

January-February 2026 already feels far away. The challenge is to be ready to surf this wave faster and better than competitors.

What this stage IS

Real transformation

This is not about flashy output. It is about transforming how we work.

Business first

Nobody is asked to become a programmer; people are asked to detect friction and opportunities.

New logic

Business identifies the opportunity, proposes output, and IT hardens, integrates and scales.

Network effect

Improvements move from arithmetic to geometric once shared across teams.

4 alignment messages

MessageExecutive reading
It is not magicIt is operational capability to redesign processes, not a tool trend.
It is not about codingIt is about business understanding, prioritization and using AI agents.
It is not optionalIgnoring this wave reduces competitiveness in speed, cost and decision quality.
Do not wait for perfect trainingInitiative is required: explore, pilot, fail fast and iterate.

Core message: DATA

  • Without correct, collected and curated data there are no sustainable AI results.
  • Data quality is critical raw material for every next step.
  • Demand for data volume and quality will keep increasing.
  • Data care must become a cross-functional discipline.
Executive conclusion: immediate advantage will not come from having more AI tools, but from stronger business criteria and higher data quality to scale pilots into solutions.
