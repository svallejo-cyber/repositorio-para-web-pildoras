From project to evidence: a living repository of AI-enabled success cases

Eva Prieto Martínez · Fifth collaborative contribution to the hub on internal knowledge, commercial activation and structured reuse of project experience

Context

The project starts from a very recognizable weakness in many organizations: the experience accumulated in real projects exists, but remains scattered across emails, presentations, conversations and individual knowledge.

The real leap is not about archiving projects better, but about turning accumulated experience into a reusable asset for marketing, sales and technical positioning.

What has been designed

The proposal is to build a living repository of success cases that allows the essential information from each project to be captured simply, structured and then automatically enriched through artificial intelligence.

The system rests on three layers: very simple capture, processing that organizes and amplifies the value of the data, and later activation for marketing, sales and internal knowledge.

What it adds to the business

Useful knowledge

It turns scattered experience into an accessible, reusable base that depends less on specific individuals.

Sales

It allows each project to become a commercial argument, a reference and evidence of accumulated experience.

Marketing

It creates a continuous source of real content for web, case studies, social media and technical positioning.

Scalability

It makes it possible for each project to produce several valuable assets from a single structured capture.

Main lessons

  • Adoption depends more on capture simplicity than on tool sophistication.
  • Mobile matters if knowledge is to be captured close to the worksite and at the right moment.
  • AI multiplies the value of data, but only when that data is properly structured.
  • Each project can generate several outputs: commercial narrative, content and internal knowledge transfer.
Collaborative conclusion: this system is not only about storing information better, but about turning Isaval’s experience into a tangible asset that can be reused, scaled and converted into competitive advantage.
