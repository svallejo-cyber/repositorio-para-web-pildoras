Collaborative 44. From Excel and email to intelligent supplier-order tracking

Author: Carmen Vicente Zaragoza

1. Current setup

Supplier-order follow-up still relies on Excel, email review and manual reading of confirmations, dates and incidents.

2. Key problems

Manual extraction from emails and PDFs, lack of real-time visibility, reactive delay management, stock-out risk, poor lead-time traceability and dependence on individual knowledge.

3. Objective

Build an internal solution that automates supplier-order tracking, centralizes key information and anticipates supply risks.

4. Proposed solution

Implement an intelligent system that combines automatic capture, continuous monitoring and proactive alerts, moving from a reactive model to a more controlled and predictive one.

5. Expected benefits

Less manual work, stronger operational visibility, earlier response to incidents, lower stock-out risk and better supplier-performance evaluation.
